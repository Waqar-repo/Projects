UK Green Energy Checker
A simple web tool that tells you how "clean" your electricity is right now based on your postcode.

What does this project do?
In the UK, the electricity in our homes comes from different sources like wind, solar, gas, or coal. Because the weather changes, the "cleanliness" of our power changes every 30 minutes.

This website allows a user to:

Enter their UK Postcode (e.g., G1, EH1, or SW1).

See a Live Rating: It shows if the carbon footprint is "Very Low," "Moderate," or "High."

See the Source: It shows exactly where the power is coming from (for example: 80% Wind, 10% Solar).
